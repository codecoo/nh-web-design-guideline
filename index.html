Design Principles

Based on Ant Design Values

  • Natural - The visual system should be derived from nature, providing users with an intuitive sense of belonging and affinity.
  • Certain - Interface design should be highly certain, reducing ambiguity and helping users make quick decisions.
  • Meaningful - Every design element should have clear meaning and purpose, avoiding decoration for decoration's sake.
  • Growing - Design systems should evolve with user needs and business requirements.
